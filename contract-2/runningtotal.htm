Refund Policy


Refunds will only be given for athletes who withdraw prior to 1st February less a $40 administration fee. Athletes withdrawing after 1st February will have their entry fee credited to any other race of their choice hosted by Media Unlimited, but Media Unlimited must be notified in writing (email is fine) prior to race day.
